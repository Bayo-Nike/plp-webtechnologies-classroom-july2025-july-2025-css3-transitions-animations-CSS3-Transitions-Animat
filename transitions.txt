
Transitions usually occur when a change of state occurs.

Transition properties
==================
1.transition-property: dectates w/c property to showcase
2.transition-duration: how long the transition should take
3.transition-timing-function: speed of the transition
    a.linear:constant speed
    b.ease: starts slow, then fast, then ends slow
    c.ease-in: starts slow
    d.ease-out: ends slow
    e.ease-in-out: starts and ends slow

4.transition-delay: delay before the transition starts



shorthand writing of a property
transition: property duration timing-function delay;


Animation properties
====================
1. @keyframes: defines the Animation
2. animation-name: name of the animation
3. animation-duration: how long the animation should take
